⚙️ One-Time Setup

Complete these steps once
1
Add a new tab to your Google Sheet named exactly Predictions with columns: PlayerID | PlayerName | MatchID | Date | Score1 | Score2 | SubmittedAt
2
Open Extensions → Apps Script in your sheet and paste the code from predict-gas.js. Click Deploy → New deployment → Web app → Anyone. Copy the URL.
3
Paste that URL into predict.html at the GAS_URL variable, and fill in the PLAYERS array with all player IDs, names, flags, and PINs.
4
Send each player their unique WhatsApp message from the panel below. They bookmark it once — done.
